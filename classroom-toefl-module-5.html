Module 5: Writing Foundation

Apply thesis-driven writing structures for integrated and independent responses.

Overview

The Writing section of the TOEFL iBT evaluates your ability to express ideas clearly, organize arguments logically, and use academic English effectively. This module builds the foundation needed to write structured, high-scoring TOEFL essays.

Students will learn how to develop strong arguments, support ideas with examples, and respond clearly to integrated tasks.
