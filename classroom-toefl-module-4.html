Module 4: Reading Foundation

Skimming, scanning, vocabulary in context, and evidence-based reading decisions.

Overview

The Reading section of the TOEFL iBT measures your ability to understand and analyze academic texts similar to those used in university courses. This module builds essential reading strategies to help students read faster, understand complex ideas, and answer questions accurately.

Strong reading skills are critical for achieving a high TOEFL score and succeeding in university-level study.
