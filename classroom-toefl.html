TOEFL Exam Framework

  • Reading: Academic passage analysis and evidence-based choices.
  • Listening: Lecture/conversation note-mapping and idea capture.
  • Speaking: Four timed tasks with structured responses.
  • Writing: Integrated and independent academic writing formats.
